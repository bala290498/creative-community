# Community Entertainment Streaming

## Overview

Our platform is a community-owned streaming ecosystem dedicated to empowering independent creators and offering audiences access to stories they won’t find on mainstream services. Unlike traditional streaming giants that prioritize large studios and commercial productions, we focus on elevating diverse voices, original ideas, and experimental formats. Through democratic participation, transparent decision-making, and fair earning opportunities, we are redefining how entertainment is created, discovered, and valued. Viewers, creators, and community members work together to build an inclusive, innovative, and culturally rich entertainment platform for all.

---

## Key Features

### Extensive Independent Content Library
A curated collection of films, documentaries, series, short-form content, and experimental works created by independent artists from around the world.

### Fair Revenue Model (80% to Creators)
Creators earn the majority of revenue generated from subscriptions and pay-per-view purchases, enabling sustainable artistic careers and equitable compensation.

### Community-Driven Governance
Users participate in shaping platform policies, content priorities, growth strategies, and feature releases through structured community voting.

### Creator Tools and Analytics
Creators gain access to intuitive dashboards, detailed performance analytics, engagement insights, scheduling tools, and monetization controls.

### Flexible and Affordable Pricing
Two subscription tiers and optional pay-per-view access make content accessible to audiences with various preferences and budgets.

### Cultural and Creative Diversity
The platform prioritizes underrepresented voices, niche genres, and culturally significant content that often goes unnoticed on mainstream services.
