# Sustainable Food Network

## Overview

The Sustainable Food Network is a community-driven platform that connects local farmers directly with consumers, creating a more sustainable and efficient food distribution system.

## Key Features

- Direct farmer-to-consumer connections
- Local pickup points for convenience
- Sustainable agriculture focus
- Community ownership model
- Transparent pricing

## Impact

Since launch, we've:
- Connected 50+ local farmers with consumers
- Processed over $200K in transactions
- Reduced food waste by 30%
- Supported 500+ active community members
